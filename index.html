BUREAU IN ACTION
"We are unable to build liberal democracies from the outside" Lord Alderdice says in speech on Afghanistan
LI President of Honour Lord John Alderdice gave a speech at the House of Lords emergency recall debate on the Taliban takeover in Afghanistan, organised 18 August. In his speech, Lord Alderdice, who has long-standing experience in conflict resolution, noted that the democracy-mission in Afghanistan was doomed to fail and that liberal democracies cannot be built through military intervention from the outside.

"Germany is entering a new political era" - LI Deputy President provides analysis of election results
The outcome of the recent elections in Germany shows that the country is entering a new political era, LI Deputy President Karl-Heinz Paqué writes in his analysis of the results. German politics are no longer ruled by two dominating parties, instead, there has been a diversification of the political spectrum. "Different programs allow society to be better represented in its particularized state. Therein lies an opportunity to effectively combat frustration with democracy."

FIGHTING FOR HUMAN RIGHTS, CLIMATE JUSTICE & FAIR TRADE
Liberal democracies must lead the defense of free speech in digital spaces: Jacob Mchangama speaks at LI's 204th ExCom
LI's Human Rights Committee was proud to welcome Jacob Mchangama, an expert on freedom of expression, to deliver a keynote presentation on free speech in the digital space and the challenges of its regulation and governance. In an engaging discussion with LI HRC Chair Astrid Thors, Mchangama described current forms of censorship as a historically observed “knee-jerk reaction” to a revolution in communications technology.
